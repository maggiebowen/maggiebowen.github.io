Our Loyalty and Mission

You might remember their smiling faces and distinct custom jewelry services from their previous work at Andrews Jewelry for roughly 40 years. Now at Benedicts Jewelers, Mary Jean Wessely and Joe Bowen’s previous hard work and passion has not been lost in their new business’ modern flair and classical taste.

After laborious years in the business, Mary Jean and Joe have built up a solid reputation, with the pair having a combined experience of over 100 years in the jewelry business. The two take pride in educating couples about diamonds and as a brother and sister duo, know the importance of making sure everyone is happy with the jewelry they purchase.

That is why they offer customized pieces, numerous options, and special appointments at your request, regardless of their hours. Their business model allows for more options than most of their competitors because they work with multiple vendors. Benedicts, alongside their professional advice, promises an overnight delivery for your approval with no obligation from the customer. Benedicts also strives to get you the best deal by comparing similar pieces across different vendors.

Mary Jean Wessely

Mary Jean previously worked at Andrews Jewelry for 36 years. She started her work in the Jewelry business in 1976 and earned her graduate gemologist degree at the Gemological Institute of America shortly after. Some might say she has a knack for jewels and gems, but we all just say she rocks at what she does.

Joe Bowen

Joe Bowen first started his work in the family jewelry business in 1950. He was just a pre-teen then and has since dedicated his life to ensuring quality service to all his customers. At one point he was even too short to reach some of the jewelry display cases, but has since grown into a jewelry professional. Because his father was his first employer and he now shares a business with his sister, quality service in this industry seems to run in his veins!
